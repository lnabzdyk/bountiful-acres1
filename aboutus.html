About Us

Mark & Sue

We bought our little piece of paradise in early 2007 and have been building the dream since then. Sue has always dreamed of owning a farm and raising animals so it has been a dream come true to add each new addition to our farm. Mark enjoys his barbershop singing and helps out on the farm when needed. He enjoys the quiet nights and skies of endless stars. In 2008 Jil, Sue's sister, came to live at the farm also. She has been an intergral part of our daily operations.

Our Farm

Our farm is located in Watertown Tennessee. At Bountiful Acres Farm we raise LaMancha and Nubian Dairy goats. We also have Shetland sheep, BFL's, Suffolk, Finn, Dorset, Cotswold, Romney and all sorts of mixes in between. We don't keep the breeds separate. I mix them to get the type of sheep I like (small and easy to handle) and the sort of wool I can spin. We also raise dairy goats, wool sheep, bees, herbs, & more. From our bounty we make soaps, lotions, body care products, herbal salves & other homemade products.
